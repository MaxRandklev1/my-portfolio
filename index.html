About Me

Hello! I'm Max Randklev and welcome to my portfolio. In here is the my favorite 45 projects that i've made over the years. This site does not encompas everything I've ever done because i've made a lot of slop, so this shows some of my best works. I have always loved to create. Through the years my process refined and evolved to where it is today. Through this portfolio you can see that evolution to an extent. The portfolio is sorted from my favorite to my least favorite, of my best works. Thanks for stopping by and I hope you enjoy your time looking at the history of me.
