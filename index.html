About Me

Learn about my journey and creative process over the years.

Hello! I'm Max Randklev and welcome to my portfolio. Here you'll find my favorite 45 projects that I've created over the years.

This site showcases some of my best works rather than everything I've ever done. I've always loved to create, and through the years my process has refined and evolved to where it is today.

Through this portfolio, you can see that evolution to an extent. The projects are sorted from my favorite to my least favorite, of my best works. Thanks for stopping by and I hope you enjoy exploring my creative journey.
